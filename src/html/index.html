The Path to Containerization

As you embark on your DevOps journey, mastering **containerization** is crucial. Technologies like Docker and container orchestrators such as Kubernetes (and its enterprise implementation, **OpenShift**) simplify application deployment and scaling. Moving from traditional virtual machines to containers provides consistency across environments—from your local machine to the Azure cloud—eliminating the dreaded "it works on my machine" problem.

The deployment process you're tackling—using **Azure Red Hat OpenShift**—is a practical demonstration of integrating infrastructure management with application delivery. This hands-on experience in building a CI/CD pipeline and managing the application lifecycle within a cloud environment is the foundation of a successful DevOps career.
